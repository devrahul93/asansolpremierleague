MESSAGE FROM FOUNDER OF A.P.L AND SECRETARY OF TRIMURTI CRICKET CLUB

In Asansol thousands of all level poor cricket players are playing cricket but golden opportunities are Less and hidden talents are surplus. Asansol is a big city for playing cricket where cricket is religion. ASANSOL PREMIER LEAGUE (A.P.L) is a big official platform for those neglected surplus talented cricket players from all over Asansol who cannot avail any opportunity to come forward and showcase their cricket talent. These Surplus players i.e. from Asansol waste their time; money and talent by playing under false associations moreover most players involve in some Bad addiction and spoil their future.

ASANSOL PREMIER LEAGUE (A.P.L) have been aiming to form Asansol based cricket franchise comprising those talented cricket players who have not been able to make it big or have been left out.

ASANSOL PREMIER LEAGUE (A.P.L)'s main aim to promote and develop the TENNIS BALL CRICKET game for men and boys from grass roots level in Asansol and provide the right opportunity as per the guidelines of ASANSOL PREMIER LEAGUE (A.P.L) i.e. official cricket league, good cricket grounds to the real talents of the Asansol.
